Request for Legal Services Module

Streamline the process of requesting legal reviews and assistance from the legal department.

Instruction to use this module.

  • Install the module in your Odoo instance.
  • Configure the module settings and user access permissions.

Key Highlights

  • Create requests for legal review of contracts, procedures, and other documents.
  • Track the progress and status of each request in real-time.
  • Collaborate with the legal department through comments and notifications.
  • Generate reports on the performance and workload of the legal department.
